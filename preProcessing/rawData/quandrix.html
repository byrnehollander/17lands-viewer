Legend

  • # Seen: The number of packs in which a card was seen. Cards that come back around "on the wheel" (i.e. picks 9 and later) are only counted once.
  • ALSA (Average Last Seen At): The average this card was last seen in a pack. When a card comes back around on the wheel, only the second time around counts toward the average. Sorting by this gives a rough sense of the overall community pick orders (or the bots' pick order for QuickDraft).
  • # Picked: The number of times 17Lands drafters picked this card.
  • ATA (Average Taken At): The average pick number at which this card was taken by 17Lands drafters. Sorting by this gives a sense of the drafters' overall pick order.
  • # GP (Number of Games Played): The number of games played with this card in the deck.
  • GP WR (Games Played Win Rate): The win rate of games where this card was in the deck. Without a lot of data, this isn't super meaningful, as it's so heavily influenced by the other cards in players' decks.
  • # OH (Number of Games in Opening Hand): The number of games where this was in the starting hand (i.e. whatever opening hand was kept).
  • OH WR (Opening Hand Win Rate): The win rate of games where this was in the starting hand. Without a lot of data, this isn't super meaningful, as it's so heavily influenced by the other cards drawn throughout the game.
  • # GD (Number of Games Drawn): The number of games where this was drawn at some point throughout the game (excluding in the opening hand).
  • GD WR (Games Drawn Win Rate): The win rate of games where this card was drawn at some point (excluding in the opening hand). Without a lot of data, this isn't super meaningful, as it's so heavily influenced by the other cards drawn throughout the game.
  • # GIH (Number of Games In Hand): The number of games where this was drawn at some point throughout the game (including in the opening hand).
  • GIH WR (Games In Hand Win Rate): The win rate of games where this card was drawn at some point (including in the opening hand). Without a lot of data, this isn't super meaningful, as it's so heavily influenced by the other cards drawn throughout the game.
  • # GND (Number of Games Not Drawn): The number of games where this was in the deck but not drawn nor in the opening hand.
  • GND WR (Games Not Drawn Win Rate): The win rate of games where this card was in the deck but not drawn nor in the opening hand. Without a lot of data, this isn't super meaningful, as it's so heavily influenced by the other cards drawn throughout the game.
  • IWD (Improvement When Drawn): The percentage point improvement in win rate when this card was drawn vs. when it was in the deck but not drawn. This is simply the difference between GIH WR and GND WR. This gives a sense of how good the card is in the decks that it's played, regardless of how the archetype performs. Without a lot of data, this isn't super meaningful, as it's so heavily influenced by the other cards drawn throughout the game.

FAQ

  • Why are some win rates empty even though there are games played, drawn, etc.?
    • We've found people try to draw conclusions where sample sizes are too small to support anything meaningful. As a result, we filter out any game win rates where the number of games is less than 800, opening hand win rates where the number of games is less than 200, and drawn card win rates where the number of games is less than 200.
  • Why are some cards missing?
    • Some cards may not have been seen by our players yet. If you want to help contribute data, check out our Getting Started page!
  • How can a card have been played in games but not be seen and/or picked?
    • It's possible to upload partial logs that include games without the draft (or vice-versa), which is probably what's happened in this case.
